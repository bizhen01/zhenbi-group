We are always looking for motivated students and researchers to join the group. The directions outlined on the [research page](/zhenbi-group/research/) are broad — strong applicants with related interests in quantum many-body physics, field theory, or quantum information are welcome to reach out even if your background is not an exact match.

## Postdoctoral researchers

Interested postdocs should email Zhen at [zjb5184@psu.edu](mailto:zjb5184@psu.edu) with:

- A current CV.
- A short (1–2 page) statement of research interests and ideas you would like to pursue.
- Names of 2–3 references.

I am happy to support applications to external fellowships (NSF MPS-Ascend, Simons, Moore, KITP, etc.) — please reach out at least a month before the fellowship deadline so we can discuss fit.

## Ph.D. students

Prospective Ph.D. students should apply to the [Penn State Physics graduate program](https://science.psu.edu/physics/graduate). Admissions are handled at the department level; I cannot admit students directly. If you are already admitted or considering applying, feel free to email me to discuss research directions.

Penn State students currently enrolled in the program who are interested in working with the group are encouraged to email me to set up a meeting.

## Undergraduate researchers

Penn State undergraduates with a strong background in quantum mechanics, statistical mechanics, and ideally some exposure to field theory or condensed matter physics are welcome to email me about projects. Independent-study and senior-thesis projects are possible.

## Visiting researchers and collaborators

If you are interested in visiting Penn State for a short collaboration, get in touch by email.
